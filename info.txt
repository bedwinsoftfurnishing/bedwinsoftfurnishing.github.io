About Us:

Curtain and blind specialists available for private and contract work. We have been in the trade for over 30 years and have a call out service where we will visit your property, measure your windows, manufacture your blinds/curtains within our own facilities and return to fit the blinds/curtains for you. We have shops Kilkeel and Warrenpoint, Co. Down, but we supply to residential and commercial customers throughout Ireland including Armagh, Down, Antrim, Derry/Londonderry, Tyrone, Louth, Meath, Monaghan, Westmeath, Cavan, Dublin, Kildare.
